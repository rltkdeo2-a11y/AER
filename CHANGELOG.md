### Validation

The model was applied to five public-sector proposal cases:

1. Financial Supervisory Service AX consulting
2. KAMCO national-property AX consulting
3. Goryeong County AX strategy research
4. National Medical Center public-healthcare AI ISP
5. Incheon Airport data-platform implementation

The following core structure remained stable across the cases:

Official information analysis

→ Customer AS-IS and TO-BE definition

→ Requirement, constraint and company-asset integration

→ Customer-effect-centered strategy generation

→ Strategy integration through common effect or causality

→ Resource allocation based on evaluation and submission constraints

→ Execution-feasibility and disqualification-risk validation

→ Information-sufficiency assessment

→ Selective reanalysis after additional information
