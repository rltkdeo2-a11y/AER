# Research Philosophy

Version: AER v1.0

Status: Frozen

---

## Purpose

This document defines the fundamental philosophy of the AX Engineering Research project.

The philosophy guides all research activities and remains stable unless a major version update is approved.

---

## Core Philosophy

We do not study AI itself.

We study organizational operating principles that remain valid in the AI era.

Technology changes.

Operating principles should remain reusable.

---

## Research Philosophy

1. Research before perfection.

The objective is to advance research, not to continuously redesign the research framework.

---

2. Separate reasoning from knowledge.

Reasoning is exploratory.

Knowledge is committed only after validation.

---

3. Evidence before conclusion.

Hypotheses may be freely generated.

Repository assets require supporting evidence.

---

4. Human remains the final decision maker.

GPT supports reasoning.

Humans approve repository changes.

---

5. Repository is the Single Source of Truth.

Conversation is temporary.

Repository assets are authoritative.

---

6. AER evolves only through practical necessity.

The framework is improved only when actual research reveals structural limitations.

---

## Research Goal

Build a practical engineering methodology for AI-enabled enterprise transformation.
