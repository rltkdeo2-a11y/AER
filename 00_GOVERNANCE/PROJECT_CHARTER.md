# Project Charter

Project Name

AX Engineering Research (AER)

---

Mission

Establish a practical engineering methodology for organizational transformation in the AI era.

---

Vision

Develop a reusable research framework that enables organizations to design, validate, and continuously evolve AI-enabled operating systems.

---

Research Scope

- AX Engineering
- Enterprise Decision Architecture
- Enterprise Memory
- Organizational Transformation
- AI-enabled Enterprise Operation

---

Out of Scope

- AI model benchmarking
- Prompt engineering techniques
- AI ethics (unless directly related to enterprise operation)

---

Research Principles

- Evidence before conclusion.
- Reasoning before commitment.
- Human approval before repository update.
- Repository is the Single Source of Truth.

---

Governance

Human Co-Researcher

- Define research objectives.
- Validate practical applicability.
- Approve Research Commits.
- Maintain repository.

GPT Co-Researcher

- Generate reasoning.
- Detect inconsistencies.
- Propose hypotheses.
- Analyze impacts.
- Generate Research Commits.
- Audit AER methodology when necessary.
