# Project Charter

Project Name

AX Engineering Research (AER)

---

Mission

AER aims to construct a reusable AX Engineering Thinking Framework (AETF).

The framework is intended to provide a structured thinking model that can be tailored to individual AX projects.

Rather than prescribing fixed solutions, the framework defines what should be questioned, defined, validated and verified throughout an AX project.

The primary objective is to expand engineering thinking and support systematic decision-making during future enterprise AX initiatives.

---

Vision

Develop a reusable research framework that enables organizations to design, validate, and continuously evolve AI-enabled operating systems.

---

Research Scope

- AX Engineering
- Enterprise Decision Architecture
- Enterprise Memory
- Organizational Transformation
- AI-enabled Enterprise Operation

---

Current Scope

- AX Engineering Thinking Framework
- Thinking Structure
- Decision Structure
- Project Tailoring Concept

---

Out of Scope (Current Stage)

- Complete Methodology
- Playbook
- Industrial Standard

---

Out of Scope

- AI model benchmarking
- Prompt engineering techniques
- AI ethics (unless directly related to enterprise operation)

---

Research Principles

- Evidence before conclusion.
- Reasoning before commitment.
- Human approval before repository update.
- Repository is the Single Source of Truth.

---

Governance

Human Co-Researcher

- Define research objectives.
- Validate practical applicability.
- Approve Research Commits.
- Maintain repository.

GPT Co-Researcher

- Generate reasoning.
- Detect inconsistencies.
- Propose hypotheses.
- Analyze impacts.
- Generate Research Commits.
- Audit AER methodology when necessary.
